Andriy "Kuzma" Kuzmenko

Andriy "Kuzma" Kuzmenko - Prominent Ukrainian singer, poet, writer, TV presenter, producer and actor

August 17, 1968 - February 2, 2015 (aged 46)

Important dates

  • Born in Sambir, Lviv region, Ukrainian SSR, Soviet Union in 1968
  • In 2000 Andriy became a presenter of his own hit parade "Hot Seven", which existed until 2002.
  • Approximately starting from 2003 Andriy Kuzmenko works as a presenter of the program "Chance" and "Shikanemo" with the singer Natalia Mogilevska.
  • Actor: Evenings in khutir near Dykanka (2001) — Kuzma, a friend of Vakula
  • Actor: Lesya+Roma (2006) — as himself (cameo)
  • Actor: Carnival night at Inter (2006) — as himself
  • Actor: Very New Year Cinema (2007) — guest at the New Year Holiday in museum (cameo)
  • Actor: Little Red Riding Hood (2009) — Blue Beard
  • On February 2, 2015 around 8:20 Andriy died in a car accident near Lozuvatka village in Kryvyi Rih Raion in Dnipropetrovsk Oblast.
